Legal Advisory Services

In today's competitive global landscape, legal advisors play a crucial role in assisting organizations with both daily operations and various corporate events across borders. We provide transparent and efficient support using leading technologies that our legal practitioners leverage to manage companies' legal activities effectively.

Our Range of services includes:
Corporate Law

Businesses encounter a multitude of legal issues stemming from daily operational activities. We offer comprehensive guidance to our clients across this spectrum of issues. With a global presence, our team seamlessly integrates global coordination with local-jurisdictional expertise to provide tailored solutions for our clients.

Among the most common ways we serve our clients are by supporting:
  • Establishment of subsidiaries, representative offices, and branch offices for foreign companies in any jurisdiction
  • Corporate compliance and regularization services
  • Maintenance of corporate records and legal bookkeeping
  • Drafting and negotiation of Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), confidentiality agreements, and settlement agreements
  • Legal advice on matters related to foreign investment
  • Corporate restructuring, conversions, dissolutions, and winding-up procedures
  • Assessment of corporate compliance and risk management
  • Preparation of board minutes, proxies, and general or special powers-of-attorney
  • Negotiation and drafting of various negotiable instruments and credit transactions
  • Incorporation of corporations and companies in various forms and characteristics
  • Review, negotiation, and drafting of diverse commercial agreements
  • Formulation of corporate policies on confidentiality, anti-corruption, and privacy
Digital Law

The landscape of digital law is in constant flux due to rapid advancements in technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Robotics, and more. These changes impact organizations of all sizes, from large enterprises to small businesses. At W&S, we blend our understanding of digital law with our proficiency in Advisory, Audit, and Taxation to offer our clients an integrated and comprehensive approach tailored to their needs.

We focus on:
  • Contract laws and private laws encompassing cloud, outsourcing, and smart contracts
  • Intellectual Property (IP) laws and Trade secrets pertaining to data, cyber, IT, e-commerce, and cloud, such as copyright, patent, and trademark laws, along with the legal aspects of IP taxation (e.g., OECD BEPS and Digital Value Chain Alignment)
  • Mandatory laws and regulations governing cyber, IT, e-commerce, and related regulatory aspects concerning privacy and data protection, as well as e-commerce regulations
Dispute Resolution

W&S Legal's dispute resolution professionals are equipped to aid multinational clients in navigating cross-border challenges, helping them steer clear of potential litigations whenever possible. In cases where litigation becomes unavoidable, our team can adeptly represent clients in court proceedings to secure favorable judgments. Leveraging our integrated network of global lawyers, we work diligently to safeguard our clients' rights and interests amidst conflicting situations

Areas of focus include:
  • Tax Disputes
  • Employment Disputes
  • Civil and Commercial Litigation and Arbitration
  • Public Litigation
Employment Law & Benefits

Businesses may encounter challenges such as reputational damage and diminished market value, which can, in turn, affect their capacity to attract and retain skilled employees, particularly concerning workforce-related matters. Our comprehensive and globally scalable employment law solutions are designed to support enterprises in addressing these issues with trust and reliability.

W&S Legal helps companies with:
  • Contingent Workforce: Assisting in developing workforce strategies and drafting contextual clauses and policies tailored to the contingent workforce.
  • Workforce Transformation: Providing consultation on downsizing or rightsizing procedures and guiding through the transformation process.
  • Compensation & Benefits: Addressing global stock plans, corporate governance, regulatory compliance, including CEO pay ratios, gender pay-gap reporting, and adherence to minimum wage requirements.
  • Individual Employment Law: Supporting with hiring processes, drafting employment contracts, handbooks, policies, and advising on termination matters.
  • Global Mobility: Managing the complexities of an increasingly internationally mobile workforce, ensuring compliance and efficiency in mobility programs.
Mergers & Acquisitions

We mitigate risks comprehensively by aligning our tax and finance professionals, thereby providing businesses with strategic guidance at every stage of transactions, from negotiation to execution.

We provide support during the following critical phases:
  • Compliance Due Diligence
  • Legal Structuring
  • Antitrust Filing/Merger Control Clearance
  • Post-M&A Integration
  • Capital Market Compliance
  • Sourcing
  • Screening
  • Due Diligence
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)
  • First Review
  • Letter of Intent (LOI) / Non-Binding Offer (NBO)
  • Binding Documentation, Completion & Closing
  • Joint Ventures and Strategic Alliances
  • Post-Merger Integration
  • Legal Project Management
Regulatory & Compliance

Businesses continually seek improved solutions to achieve organizational goals, enhance specialization, and deepen sector-specific knowledge amidst the heightened complexities of global regulatory challenges. At W&S, we merge our global insights with local regulations to assist companies in developing and implementing tailored solutions to navigate these challenges effectively.

Our regulatory and compliance services provide support to clients in areas such as:
  • Antitrust, Competition, and Trade
  • Sanctions Compliance
  • Financial Services Regulation and Investigations
  • Business Integrity and Financial Crime
  • Insurance Regulation
  • Energy Sector Compliance
  • Public Law Affairs
Private Clients Legal Services

The W&S Private Client Legal Services team supports private businesses and individuals worldwide in navigating unique challenges and seizing growth, innovation, expansion, and other opportunities. Leveraging practitioners from across our global network, we deliver insightful advice to private businesses and individuals globally.

Our practitioners tailor the full scope of business law services for:
  • High-net-worth individuals
  • Small, high-growth enterprises
  • Private equity funds/houses and their portfolio companies
  • Family-run businesses and their family offices
  • Privately-owned and small cap businesses
Real Estate

The W&S Legal Services team caters to a diverse range of construction companies and real estate investors. With the requisite expertise and experience, we offer comprehensive services to real estate clients across different countries worldwide.

Services include:
  • Lease and sublease agreements
  • Registration services
  • Development, building, and planning
  • Litigation support
  • Ownership restructuring
  • Real estate transactions and due diligence
  • Financing and syndication
Commercial Law

Collaborating closely with Financial Advisors, Accounting, and Tax practitioners, W&S Legal offers comprehensive legal solutions tailored to banking and securities. Our services encompass various financial transactions and funding arrangements, including operating and finance agreements and intra-group funding.

Commercial Law

As regulations become more stringent and local enforcements tighter, navigating them can be challenging. At W&S Legal, our team brings a practical focus to commercial law solutions. Collaborating closely with our multidisciplinary team, we offer a holistic perspective to key sectors and industries, ensuring comprehensive and effective legal support.

Services include:
  • Comprehensive Pre-Insolvency Strategies
  • Thorough Transfer Pricing Documentation and Intra-Group Contracting
  • Strategic Commercial Contracting
  • Professional Commercial Advisory Services
