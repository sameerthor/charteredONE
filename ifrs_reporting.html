IFRS Reporting

IFRS has supplanted numerous national accounting standards worldwide but has not replaced the distinct accounting standards in the United States, where US GAAP is utilized. Companies with international operations encounter a multitude of challenges. Apart from variations in culture and preferences, regulatory and accounting demands can also vary significantly. IFRS serves as the universal financial reporting language. Recent major standards concerning financial instruments, revenue recognition, and leasing pose significant challenges for financial statement preparers.

Our Solutions Include
  • Preparation of Individual and Consolidated Financial Reports adhering to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (US GAAP)
  • Assisting companies in comprehending, implementing, and evaluating the impact of intricate accounting and reporting modifications stemming from evolving regulations and new accounting standards, as well as shifts in business structure
  • Evaluating the implications for other strategic initiatives within your organization (e.g., Quality Close, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), and other system integration and upgrade endeavors)
  • Offering comprehensive support throughout the accounting process, including process integration and financial reporting, for mergers, acquisitions, IPOs, and divestitures
  • Development of new IFRS accounting policies and procedures
  • Conducting gap analysis between required information and current availability
  • Conducting financial reporting comparisons with a selected peer group
  • Preparation of accounting manuals, accounting policy papers, and accounting position papers
  • Tailored training sessions and update sessions featuring practical examples and case studies
