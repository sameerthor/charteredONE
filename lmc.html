Legal Management Consulting

W&S's Legal Management Consulting services were crafted by legal professionals in partnership with experts spanning management consultancy, risk advisory, and tax management consulting. LMC comprises models, frameworks, and systems aimed at assisting in-house lawyers in enhancing the efficiency of their departments and meeting their business objectives.

Our services include:
Legal Operating Model

Different organizational events, such as mergers, shifts in business strategy, or cost reduction challenges, often necessitate a change in the legal operating model. W&S Legal offers assistance to organizations seeking to modify their legal operating models, whether through comprehensive transformations or targeted assessments of specific areas.

  • Decrease legal expenditures
  • Allow the legal team to concentrate on critical and strategic endeavors
  • Enhance efficiency through process enhancements and technology integration
  • Update risk-management procedures
  • Enhance service delivery and minimize delays
Legal Process

Numerous internal and external factors offer opportunities for organizations to evolve their current legal departments. W&S's legal process advisors aid clients in crafting systematic, intuitive, and efficient processes. We can support clients' legal processes in the following key areas:

  • Management of client contracts
  • Management of supplier contracts
  • Preparation for dawn raids
  • Compliance protocol establishment
  • Corporate governance oversight
  • Management of litigation
Legal Risk and Matter Management

Establishing a defined risk framework and strategy to anticipate critical legal risks is preferable to dealing with crises later on. At W&S, we closely collaborate with our clients and legal professionals to conduct comprehensive risk assessments, perform gap analyses, and develop roadmaps to address any identified gaps. We assist in managing legal risks in the following key areas:

  • Contract Management
  • Litigation/Disputes
  • Legislation Changes
  • Data Privacy
Legal Sourcing

W&S Legal can assist clients in developing a model that achieves more with fewer resources by striking a balance between in-house capabilities, technology, and external service providers.

W&S Legal can assist General Counsels and their teams in selecting and implementing technology tailored to their needs and objectives, customized to their staff and their aims.

Legal Technology

Technology offers the potential to mitigate risk with enhanced efficiency and reliability, automate mundane and repetitive tasks, and liberate the team to concentrate on strategic priorities, enabling greater productivity with fewer resources.

W&S Legal can assist General Counsels and their teams in selecting and implementing technology tailored to their needs and objectives, customized to their staff and their aims.

We help them:
  • Gain insight into available options
  • discern the most suitable solutions
  • craft a legal technology infrastructure capable of delivering value across the enterprise
